general macros
keywords: macros, general, misc, keystrokes, shortcuts, hotkeys
description: General hotkeys for playing prometheus.
These are general macros to make your game play more comfortable. Please also take a look at, sphelp keypad and sphelp configuration to learn about the more complex keypad navigation and shortcuts for volume, filter and so on.

Alt+c - prints the current coordinates.
Alt+shift+c - prints the current galactic coordinates.
alt+h - prints your current hp.
alt+g - prints your current cash.
alt+x - prints current status and points, if not raisable
alt+. - prints hunger and thirst information
alt+e - List the exits available at your character's current location.
